Escape From Project E.L.E.V.A.T.E.

About this Game

....Rebooting System for Model Name: HOPE, System ID: CM17PM2S2023...

... Running Damage Analysis... Walk System Failure. Changed to Hover Support System....Grapple Tether in Functioning Condition.

"Where am I? Where is everyone else?"

....Allow me to inform you HOPE. Project E.L.E.V.A.T.E. had an accident 3 years ago. All of the other robots were put out of commission. The facility you are trapped in is the lowest area of E.L.E.V.A.T.E., which the public doesn't know. Traverse through the area to find a way out of here. The answer should lie there.

Guide Test Subject HOPE throughout the remainder of project ELEVATE! Use your grapple gun to zip around each of the levels, go through each puzzle and make it to the end! Find out about what happened in this area, and possibly, newer secrets never known before...

Main Mechanics:

  • Use the grapple gun to traverse through the remains of E.L.E.V.A.T.E.!
  • The Green Zip Block will move you directly toward it!
  • Use The Jump Pads to rise up to high areas!
  • Watch out for hazards! They will make you restart...

Controls:

  • W / SHIFT - Retract/Retract with Boost
  • S / CTRL - Extend/Extend with Boost
  • A and D - Swing/Move Left and Right
  • Left Click - Grapple Towards Mouse
  • Space / Right Click - Release Grapple
  • Arrow Keys: Quick Grapple
  • R - Reset

The Compact Edition of this game focuses on experiencing the main gameplay loop, so the narrative is abbreviated and not included. The edition will contain 10 levels you can choose from. Beating Level 10 will get you to a victory screen before returning back to the main menu.
